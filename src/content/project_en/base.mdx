**Key Features:**

1. **Member Management:** Allows adding, editing, and deleting members, recording information such as name, surname, address, email, phone number, etc.

2. **Member Classification:** Enables categorizing members based on their status (member or supporter), level of engagement, geographic location, etc.

3. **Communication:** Provides an internal messaging system to facilitate communication between members and EELV officials.

4. **Events:** Allows creating and promoting events, meetings, conferences, etc., and tracking member participation.

5. **Statistics and Reports:** Generates reports and statistics based on member data, providing valuable insights for decision-making and analysis.

6. **Authentication and Security:** Ensures secure authentication to ensure only authorized users can access sensitive information.

7. **User-Friendly Interface:** Offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface for easy use by EELV members.

**Advantages:**

- **Improved Management:** Facilitates the management of EELV members, reducing administrative workload and enhancing operational efficiency.

- **Community Engagement:** Promotes member engagement and active participation through transparent communication and event promotion.

- **Data Analysis:** Allows analyzing member data to better understand their profiles and needs.

- **Data Security:** Ensures the security of sensitive member data through robust security measures.
